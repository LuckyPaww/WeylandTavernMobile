Welcome to Weyland Tavern!
Hey! Welcome to Weyland Tavern's mobile port, powered by BapOS! I'm Kressa, Weyland University's support staff~

Before we install anything on your phone, you deserve a proper briefing. So, real quick...
  • Weyland Tavern is a private roleplay platform with a whole cast of characters and campus lore, lovingly maintained by the Weyland team.
  • Everything runs locally on your device, but API calls (Sonnet, Gemini, etc) are sent across the Weyland backend for processing unless you're using your own API provider. Your chatlogs themselves live on your phone, not on somebody's server!
  • Weyland Tavern is a custom, heavily modified distro of SillyTavern, the wonderful open-source project by Cohee, RossAscends and the SillyTavern community. We stand on their shoulders and we're very grateful for it!
  • This app installs the Tavern, keeps it updated, and runs the whole server for you. No terminal, no scary commands — you tap buttons and Bap, our adorable antichrist catgirl, does the rest.

That's the tour! Next screen goes over the one-time setup. See you inside!~  —Kressa
Welcome to Weyland Tavern Mobile!
Hi there! 🐾 Before the fun starts, BapOS needs to set up Weyland Tavern on this phone. This happens once. Here's exactly what it will do:
1.Download Weyland Tavern from our official GitHub (~700 MB — WiFi strongly recommended!)
2.Unpack everything into the app's private storage (needs ~2 GB free)
3.Enroll you at Weyland University ✨
🔊 Heads up: BapOS uses sound effects! Lower your volume if you're in public.
Future launches skip all this. Updates only download what changed.
